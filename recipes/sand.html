Ham and egg sandwhich

Ingredients

  • Brioche marble bread sliced or in a loaf
  • Ham of your choosing I prefer Honey Ham
  • Eggs
  • Provolone

    • Sauce

    You do not have to include this sauce, the sandwhich is fantastic without it. Here is the one I reccomend.

    Bechamel Sauce

Preperation

  1. If you are planning of making the sauce prepare that first by following the instructions on the link above.
  2. Get two sautee pans out one large one small and one skillet. Set them on the stove on Medium high heat.
  3. Slice your bread or just put your pre sliced bread on your larger pan after coating with butter.
  4. Then spray your small pan with no stick spray and crack two eggs cook to your desired temp I prefer over medium for this sandwhich.
  5. Sear both side of your Ham in your skillet. Then add two slices of cheese and pour 2 tablespoons of water in your pan and cover with a lid. set temp to low and leave till cheese is fully melted.
  6. Take your toasted bread and cover with sauce if you chose to make it if not grab your eggs and ham and lay them on your bread.
  7. Take a big bite and enjoy.
