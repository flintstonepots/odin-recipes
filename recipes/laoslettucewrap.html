Laos Lettuce Wrap

Ingredients

  • Rib Eye steak or steak of choice
  • Romaine lettuce or bibb lettuce
  • Vietnamese vermicelli noodles
  • Mint
  • Cilantro

    • Sauce Ingredients

  • Fish sauce or Fermented fish sauce for a earthier flavor
  • Garlic
  • Palm sugar
  • Thai chillis
  • Lime

    • Compound butter

  • Salted butter
  • Lemon grass
  • Minced garlic or fresh garlic
  • Garlin bullion paste

    Preperation

  1. Let steak sit out not in its package for atleast 30 min to bring it to room temp
  2. Set your oven to 400 degrees and get a soup pot 3/4th's of the way full with hot water and set that on medium high heat till it boils
  3. For this step you will need a Laos style mortar and pestle. You will first crush the Palm sugar, Garlic and thai chillis by pounding them in the mortar and pestle.
  4. After those are thoroughly mash add quater cup of fish sauce and the juice of one lime mix it around till throughly combined and pour into serving ramekin
  5. Add your noodles to your boiling water. Cook till al dente drain and rinse with cold water plate them on a serving dish of your choice after throughly cooled and fairly dry
  6. Wash your lettuce and dry it then plate it on a serving dish. If you chose romaine lettuce, cut off the butt of the lettuce. and peel off each leaf and througly wash dirt tends to get stuck in them. After they are dry plate them on a serving dish of your choice.
  7. Wash and dry your mint and cilantro than plate those on a serving dish of your choice
  8. Get a nice cast iron skillet or steel fry pan or a grill pan place that on the stovetop on high heat.
  9. Melt butter in a small bow for 30 seconds in microwave pull out add your garlic mashed or minced your garlic bullion and your lemon grass throughly combine and set in freezer to set
  10. Salt and pepper your steaks on one side to match the thickness of your steaks. Than place your steaks in your pan till you get a nice sear. While searing the first side salt and pepper the next. Now flip your steak over and sear it on that side and do not forget to render the fat on the sides of your steak espcially if you got rib eye. Take the steaks off place them on a cutting board. empty your pan of grease and wipe it out. turn your heat down low and place your steaks back. Add your compound butter and baste for 30 seconds than place your steak into your over untill internal temparature reaches 144 fahrenheit or whatever temp you like your steak
  11. pull out your steak let it rest for 5 to 10 min before thinly slicing best way to serve is to just leave it on the cutting board and place the whole board with the rest of your Serving platters.
  12. Sit down and enjoy by building your wraps with however much of each item you wish.
