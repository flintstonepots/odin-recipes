Rice and fish cake ramen

Ingredients

  • Neo Guri spicy ramen
  • Yuki Toppogi rice cakes
  • Fish Cakes
  • Green onions
  • Soy sauce and oyster sauce

    Preperation

  1. Place a medium sized sauce pot on the stove with cold water and fill halfway. Place desired amount of rice and fish cake in and let soak for 15 minutes.
  2. Turn the heat to high and bring to a boil.
  3. Chop your green onions. Then add your green onion roots to the pot.
  4. Put your noodles in and let them cook for 5 min.
  5. pour out your desired amount of water. then add your sauces and seasoning packet.
  6. Serve in a large soup bowl and enjoy. can top with extra green onions.
